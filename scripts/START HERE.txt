PingMenuBar — read this first
=============================

macOS blocks unsigned apps downloaded from the internet.
Double-clicking PingMenuBar (or any helper script) will fail with:

  “Apple could not verify PingMenuBar is free of malware…”

That system dialog cannot be changed. Do not double-click the app to open it
the first time.


What to do (about 15 seconds)
-----------------------------

1. Drag PingMenuBar to the Applications folder (right side of this window).

2. Open Terminal:
     • Press Cmd + Space
     • Type: Terminal
     • Press Return

3. Copy the NEXT LINE entirely, paste it into Terminal, press Return:

xattr -cr /Applications/PingMenuBar.app && open /Applications/PingMenuBar.app

4. PingMenuBar should appear in your menu bar.
   From now on, open it normally from Applications or Spotlight.


Optional: full installer script
-------------------------------
If the app is still only on this disk (not yet in Applications), run:

bash "/Volumes/PingMenuBar/install.sh"

Or drag install.sh into the Terminal window and press Return.


If Terminal is not an option
----------------------------
1. Double-click PingMenuBar once (the warning appears — click Done).
2. Open “Privacy & Security” on this disk (or System Settings → Privacy & Security).
3. Scroll down and click Open Anyway for PingMenuBar.
