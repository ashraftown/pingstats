PingMenuBar — first open (unsigned release)
==========================================

macOS may block the app the first time with:
  “Apple could not verify PingMenuBar is free of malware…”

That dialog cannot be customized. Releases are not Apple-notarized.

Easiest (recommended)
---------------------
1. Drag PingMenuBar to Applications (if you have not already).
2. Double-click “Open PingMenuBar” on this disk.
3. If Terminal asks to run the script, choose Open / Allow.
4. The helper clears quarantine and launches the app.
5. Later opens: double-click PingMenuBar in Applications as usual.

If the helper is also blocked
-----------------------------
Open Terminal and paste:

  xattr -cr /Applications/PingMenuBar.app && open /Applications/PingMenuBar.app

Press Return.

Manual Gatekeeper path
----------------------
1. Try to open PingMenuBar once (the warning appears).
2. System Settings → Privacy & Security.
3. Scroll down and click Open Anyway for PingMenuBar.
